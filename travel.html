Favorite Places

Menorca, Spain

While most flock to Ibiza for its famous discos and beaches, my heart is in Menorca. It's the lesser known Balearic Island and I hope it stays that way. Although I have so many places to see, I always find myself returning to its picture perfect beaches, white rocky cliffs, and crystal clear water.

Recommendations

  • Swim for hours in Cala Pregonda
  • Take a long hike to an empty cala
  • Order too much seafood in Fornells
  • Explore Cuitadella and its port
  • Take in the views from Monte Toro
  • Try the famous Mahón Gin

Paris, France

What can I say? Rain or shine, Paris is the most beautiful city in the world. While tourists try to visit every famous monument and museum in 3 days, I recommend skipping sites like the Louvre and Champs-Elysees. Instead, enjoy a long walk off the busy streets with stops at dreamy cafés.

Recommendations

  • Relax in Parc Monceau
  • Buy lots of cheese from any fromagerie
  • Go Shopping in Le Marais
  • Dine at L'Avant Comptoir
  • Wander the Marché aux Puces de St-Ouen
  • Get lost in the City of Lights

Hong Kong

I didn't stay long enough in Hong Kong to get to know it well, and I've been anxious to return ever since I boarded the plane to leave. Its iconic skyline, colorful traditions, and legendary dining is not easy to forget.

Recommendations

  • Take in the views from the Star Ferry
  • Visit the Temple Street Night Market
  • Look down from Victoria Peak
  • Eat dim sum at a local restaurant
  • Visit Po Lin Monastery and the Tian Tan Buddha
  • Cocktails in Soho
